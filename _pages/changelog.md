# Version 1.0
## Welcome to Bloom Partner
**Version 1.0 – March 2026**

Bloom Partner is the companion app for Bloom — giving partners a real-time, read-only window into her cycle, pregnancy, or postpartum journey.

---

### Connect in Seconds
- Enter the 6-character code generated by your partner in the Bloom app
- No account, no sign-up, no Apple ID required
- Connects to her live snapshot via iCloud — data appears instantly

### Cycle & Fertility Dashboard
- See her current cycle phase — Menstrual, Follicular, Ovulation, or Luteal
- Fertile window and ovulation day highlighted clearly
- Day of cycle and cycle length at a glance

### Pregnancy Dashboard
- Current week and trimester
- Baby size comparison updated each week
- Days until due date with a visual progress bar
- Upcoming appointment dates

### Postpartum Dashboard
- Weeks postpartum since birth
- Last feeding session summary
- Current mood and recovery progress bar
- 12-week recovery milestone view

### Partner Notifications
- Period started
- Ovulation day
- Fertile window opens
- New pregnancy week milestone
- Due date in 7 days
- Baby born — postpartum begins
- Mood logged as sad or anxious

### Privacy First
- She controls what's shared via a 6-character code that expires after 30 days
- Her data never leaves her device — you only see what she explicitly shares
- No account required on either end
- Disconnect at any time
