# Privacy Policy for Bloom Partner

**Last Updated: March 28, 2026**

## Introduction

Bloom Partner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Bloom Partner mobile application (the "App").

**Location**: Warsaw, Poland
**Contact**: osanuo.studio@gmail.com

---

## What Bloom Partner Does

Bloom Partner is a read-only companion app. Your partner shares a 6-character code from the Bloom app, and Bloom Partner uses that code to display a snapshot of her cycle, pregnancy, or postpartum data — privately and in real time.

Bloom Partner **never writes, modifies, or uploads any health data**. It only reads a snapshot that your partner has explicitly chosen to share.

---

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Bloom Partner stores only the following on your device:

- **Share Code**: The 6-character code you entered, saved in local device storage (UserDefaults) so you don't need to re-enter it
- **Notification Preferences**: Whether you have granted notification permission

### Data Fetched from the Cloud

When you connect with a share code, Bloom Partner fetches a partner snapshot from Apple's CloudKit public database. This snapshot was created and shared by your partner from the Bloom app. It may include:

- Current cycle phase, fertile window, and ovulation status
- Pregnancy week, trimester, baby size, and due date
- Postpartum recovery week and feeding/mood summaries
- Upcoming appointment dates

This data is fetched on demand and is never stored persistently on your device beyond the current app session.

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect your name, email, or any personal information
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your contacts or Apple Health
- We do NOT collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT share any data with third parties
- We do NOT use cookies or similar tracking technologies

---

## How the Share Code Works

The 6-character share code is generated by your partner in the Bloom app. It maps to a record in Apple's CloudKit public database containing a snapshot of the data she chooses to share. The record expires automatically after 30 days (rolling) unless renewed by your partner.

You can disconnect at any time from within the app, which removes the share code from your device.

---

## Apple CloudKit

Bloom Partner reads data from Apple's CloudKit public database. This does not require you to have an Apple ID or iCloud account. The data retrieved is limited to what your partner has chosen to share. CloudKit is managed by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

---

## Notifications

Bloom Partner may send you local notifications when the partner snapshot updates — for example, when her period starts, ovulation day arrives, a new pregnancy week begins, or mood is logged as needing support.

- Notifications are scheduled locally on your device
- No notification content is sent to external servers
- You can disable notifications at any time in **Settings → Notifications → Bloom Partner**

---

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Since all data processing happens locally on your device or via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure, we process data based on:

- **Consent**: You voluntarily enter a share code and grant notification permission
- **Contractual Necessity**: To provide the App's core functionality you requested

---

## Data Storage and Security

- The share code is stored using iOS's standard local storage (UserDefaults)
- No personal or health data is written to your device's persistent storage
- All CloudKit communication is encrypted by Apple

---

## Third-Party Services

Bloom Partner does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's CloudKit for reading the partner snapshot.

---

## Children's Privacy

Bloom Partner is intended for use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## Your Rights and Choices

### Disconnect

You can disconnect from your partner's data at any time using the Disconnect button in the app. This removes the share code from your device.

### Disable Notifications

You can turn off notifications at any time in:
- The App settings
- Your device's **Settings → Notifications → Bloom Partner**
