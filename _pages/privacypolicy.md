# Privacy Policy for Bloom

**Last Updated: March 23, 2026**

## Introduction

Bloom ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Bloom mobile application (the "App").

**Developer**: Dawid (Osanuo)
**Location**: Warsaw, Poland
**Contact**: osanuo.studio@gmail.com

---

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Bloom stores the following information locally on your iOS device:

- **Cycle Data**: Period dates, cycle lengths, and predicted phases
- **Daily Logs**: Symptoms, mood, energy levels, and notes you enter
- **Pregnancy Records**: LMP date, due date, weight entries, kick counts, contractions, and symptoms
- **Postpartum Records**: Recovery weeks, mood entries, feeding sessions, and sleep data
- **Appointments**: Dates, titles, and notes for health check-ups
- **Contraception Records**: Method, start date, and reminder preferences
- **Health Import Data**: Cycle and fertility data imported from Apple Health (if you choose to import)
- **User Preferences**: App settings, current mode, notification preferences, and premium status

### Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

- We do NOT collect your personal information (name, email, phone number)
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your contacts
- We do NOT collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
- We do NOT send your health data to external servers
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT share your data with third parties
- We do NOT use cookies or similar tracking technologies

---

## Apple Health Integration

Bloom can optionally read and write health data from Apple Health (HealthKit):

- **Read**: Menstrual cycle data, reproductive health events, heart rate, and activity data — only if you explicitly grant permission
- **Write**: Cycle, period, fertility, and reproductive health data — only if you explicitly grant permission
- All HealthKit data remains on your device and within Apple's secure HealthKit framework
- You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Bloom**

---

## How We Use Your Information

All data collected by Bloom is used solely to:

- Track your cycle, fertility, pregnancy, and postpartum recovery
- Calculate predictions, phases, and health insights
- Send you reminders (only if you enable them)
- Sync data across your Apple devices via iCloud (if you enable iCloud sync)
- Display widgets on your Home Screen and Lock Screen

---

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Since all data processing happens locally on your device under your direct control, we process your data based on:

- **Consent**: You voluntarily choose to enter health data and grant permissions
- **Contractual Necessity**: To provide the App's core functionality you requested
- **Legitimate Interests**: To enable core features like cycle predictions and health insights

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using:

- **iOS Secure Storage**: Apple's secure storage mechanisms (SwiftData)
- **Encryption**: Your data is protected by iOS's built-in encryption when your device is locked
- **No Cloud Servers**: We do not maintain any servers that store your personal data
- **App Groups**: Widget data is shared between the app and widget extension via a secure local App Group container

### iCloud Sync

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

- Your health data is synced across your Apple devices using your personal iCloud account
- This data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud storage
- We cannot access your iCloud data
- This is entirely managed by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the App settings

---

## Third-Party Services

Bloom does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

If you purchase a subscription through the App Store:

- Payment is processed entirely by Apple

- Your purchase is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

### Access Your Data
All your data is visible within the App at all times.

### Delete Your Data
You can delete your data at any time by:

- Using "Reset App Data" in the Profile tab settings
- Deleting the App from your device (this permanently removes all local data)

### Export Your Data
You can export your health data from the App's Settings as a CSV file (Premium feature).

### Disable Notifications
You can turn off notifications at any time in:

- The App's Settings
- Your device's **Settings → Notifications → Bloom**

### Revoke HealthKit Access
You can revoke Health access at any time in:

- **Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Bloom**

## GDPR Compliance (European Economic Area Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have enhanced data protection rights under GDPR.

### Your GDPR Rights

- **Right of Access**: You can view all your data within the App at any time
- **Right to Rectification**: You can edit your health data at any time
- **Right to Erasure**: You can delete all data using "Reset App Data" in settings
- **Right to Data Portability**: You can export your data as CSV (Premium)
- **Right to Object**: You can choose not to enable optional features like notifications or iCloud sync
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: You can disable any optional features at any time

### Data Protection Authority
